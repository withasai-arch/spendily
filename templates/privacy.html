Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information Spendly collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your name and email address. While using the app, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. We use your email to authenticate your account and, if applicable, to send you important service updates. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your financial data is private to your account and is never disclosed to other users or external organizations.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, including secure storage and access controls. However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your account password confidential.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. Deleting your account will permanently remove all associated expense records from our system.
